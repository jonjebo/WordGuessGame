Hangman Game!

Rules:

1. Press any key to get started!

2. Try to guess the words based on the amount of blanks on screen.

3. Do not die! Your amount of guesses will be dispalyed on screen and when they are at "0" you lose.

4. Your guesses will be displayed on screen so do not guess the some thing more than once.

5. If you manage to guess the word then, YOU WIN! Good Luck!
